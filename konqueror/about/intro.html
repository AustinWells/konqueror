Introduction

Welcome to Konqueror.

With Konqueror you have your filesystem at your command, browsing local or networked drives with equal ease. Thanks to the component technology used throughout KDE 2, Konqueror is also a full featured, easy to use, and comfortable Web Browser, which you can use to explore the internet.

Simply enter the internet address (e.g. http://www.kde.org) of the webpage you want and press enter. Or choose one of the entries in your bookmark-menu. If you want to go back to the previous webpage, press the button   ("back") in the toolbar. To go back to the home-directory of your local filesystem press  ("Home").
